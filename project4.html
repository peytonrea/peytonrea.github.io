Sweet Melodies, Savory Flavours

Editorial, Book Design, Typesetting, Photography

This product is for the fictional client Rare Earth.

I began my process by adapting an earlier sketch I had done of general grain products to fit my new ancient grain pasta route of rolling hills, a sunset, and a combine harvesting a field of wheat. I decided to predominate showcase the product through a window that takes the shape of the sun.

For the final artwork, I stuck with warm colour tones such as browns, reds, and oranges to match the colour of most harvested grains. For the hierarchy, I made the window showcasing the product the largest so consumers will instantly know what it is theyâ€™re buying with just one glance. I then made the company branding the second largest so repeat consumers will recognize the branding and establish a positive brand association. The product name comes next so people know what type of pasta is behind the window.

Next are the benefits which include that the pasta is a high source of fiber, it is 100% organic, and that it takes only 5 minutes to cook. And the final piece of the hierarchy is the regulatory information which is located at the bottom of the box.

This piece displays my knowledge on the required legal informations for food packaging in Canada as well as my ability to typeset in multiple languages.

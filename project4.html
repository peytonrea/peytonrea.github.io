Pairing Book

For this project, I was tasked with compiling, photographing, and designing a book that pairs any two things I came up with the idea of pairing vinyl records recipes based on the length of the album and how long it takes to cook each recipe; the idea being you would put on an album and listen to it while cooking and having both wrap up sententiously. 

 Over several days I took photos of my vinyl records along with their food pairings and kept in mind the staging, lighting, focus, and background to keep all the shots looking like they were from the same shoot, then lightly edited them where needed. I then began creating layouts with my photos and the copy for the recipe. I decided to go for a horizontal book layout as they often tend to stay open better which is very important and helpful when cooking. 

I then settled on the name ‘Sweet Melodies, Savoury Flavours’ and ordered the book around the theme of sweet and savoury. As part of my book design I created a fictional publishing company called New Quill Publishing and I designed a logo for them of a fuill outline filled with liquid ink that makes up the second ‘L’ in quill.

Hi! I'm Peyton.

I am currently enrolled in my second year in Conestoga College’s Graphic Design program and I have graduated from the Design Foundations course at the same school. I am a student member of the Association of Registered Graphic Designers and participated in Creative Day 4 Social Good hosted by Capacity Canada where I worked with a team to develop new branding for Wellbeing Waterloo for charity with an intense turnaround time. 

I have also worked with several clients such as Cedarview Forestry, Healing Through Touch, and the City of Cambridge to produce branding and web-design projects. 

Creativity has always been one of my strongest assets. From a young age I have been exploring new opportunities to create from building houses for Barbies out of recycled boxes to embroidering custom patches for friends; there isn’t a medium that I won’t use to my advantage when it comes to design. 

I am well versed in both Mac and Windows operating systems and am proficient with the Adobe Creative Suite, the Microsoft Office Suite, and Procreate and I have a foundation in HTML & CSS.

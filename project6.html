Toyota Mural

This piece was part of a client project with Toyota Motor Manufacturing Canada to design a mural for the training facilities at the Cambridge plant.

The concept was to create a geometric outline of the Lexus 2021 IS using triangular shapes. These shapes create a sense of depth which will make the Lexus appear as if it is coming out from the wall. Triangles are a shape know for itâ€™s stability which will reflect the stability and work ethic of the employees as wellas the sturdiness of Toyota vehicles.

The 7 triangle shapes at the bottom hold a representation of the different departments and their importance, having an icon to represent each.The colour red is not only already associated with the Toyota brand, but it also represents the passion of Toyota employees and customers for their vehicles.

 

This piece displays my ability to take in feedback from a client through several iterations to develop a concept to the end. It also demonstrates my ability to work around design constraints such as the locations of objects like the doors within the facility.

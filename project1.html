Hill & Sons Beer Can Branding 

I was tasked with designing the branding identity as well as 3 product offerings for the fictional company ‘Hill & Sons Brew Co.’

 The first step in develop the branding for Hill & Sons came when I was researching target audiences of craft beer. I found a Texas Tech University study that broke down the craft beer drinking demographics and one smaller group labelled the ‘Conservatives’ interested me. They are a demographic of 21-40 year old women who are risk-takers and commonly seek out novelty flavours. I chose them as my target audience and geared my flavours, branding and hero-graphics towards them by directly depicting 3 diverse women with fruit for hair. 

Green, orange, and yellow were used to match the colour of each fruit to establish an immediate colour-flavour association and because they are genderneutral colours, they will help ease more women into craft-beerdrinking spaces. 

I chose vintage inspired fonts for the flavour names and product identification because of the bold and energetic feelings they evoke and because vintage trends are popular with my target audience of young-adult to middle-aged women.

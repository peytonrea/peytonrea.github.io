Jamaroo: Jam Jars

While learning about Canada's packaging guidelines, I was tasked with creating a regulation-compliant jam packaging for 2 different flavours of jam. The two flavours are form the same fictional company, Sweetberry Farms, and appear as a set.

 While drafting ideas, I came up with the product name "Jamaroo" and decided that a kangaroo character would pair nicely for a target audience of parents of young children. I created a child-friendly flat illustration of a kangaroo character and shifted it's colours depending on the flavour. The kangaroo is also holding the fruit the flavour is based on. 

 To keep the label compliant with Canadian packaging regulations, I made all the text bilingual and included a nutrition and  ingredient breakdowns, a weight statement, a UPC code, and a domicile.

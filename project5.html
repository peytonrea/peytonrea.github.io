Toyota Mural

Mural Design, Client Work, Partner project with Alexa Urbina Miranda

This piece was part of a client project with Toyota Motor Manufacturing Canada to design a mural for the training facilities at the Cambridge plant. The mural consists of pieces of the wall appearing as geometric shapes that build the shape of a car, and icons to represent the 7 stages of car assembly.

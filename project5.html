Restaurant Branding

The branding for a fictional restaurant called The Garden Bowl. The restaurant is geared towards teen girls.

While developing the branding for “The Garden Bowl” I tried to hone in on eating habits/ colour choices and type trends among teens to ensure my branding speak to my target audience

This work displays my ability to create a cohesive branding system that is expandable across many different objects and layouts.
